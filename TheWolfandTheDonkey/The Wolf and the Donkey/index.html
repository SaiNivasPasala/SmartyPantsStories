A long, long time ago, there was a WOLF.

He was really hungry. As he wandered around the forest he saw a DONKEY.


The wolf CREPT up silently behind the donkey, but the donkey had noticed him. So the donkey thought up a clever way to SAVE his life.

The donkey started crying. The wolf went up to the donkey and asked why he was crying. “I have a THORN in my foot and it hurts really bad, but I can’t get it out”, the donkey replied.

“I can help you! If I eat you now, it will stop hurting!” said the wolf. The donkey said “That’s a good idea, but if you eat, the thorn may get STUCK in your throat later”.

“But if you REMOVE the thorn first and eat me, everything will be alright", he added.

The wolf thought that it sounded good and AGREED to take the thorn out.














The donkey had been waiting for the wolf to bend over. He KICKED the wolf so hard that the wolf fell down and couldn’t get up. The donkey then RAN away as fast as he could.




                MORAL




Always use your presence of mind to take care of difficult situations
