One warm summer’s day a FOX was ambling along, enjoying the sunshine. He came across a vine.






Juicy bunches of GRAPES were hanging from its branches, ripe and ready to be eaten.

Walking back a few steps, the fox took a run up and JUMPED and almost reached the grapes.

He took a few steps back and ran and jumped again, but he still COULDN'T reach the grapes.




He TRIED again and again until he became very tired and couldn’t jump any more.



The fox thought for a while, put his nose in the air and said to himself, “Well, I’m sure the grapes were sour anyway!”




                MORAL



It is not easy to like what you cannot get.
